Pig dice

Pig dice is a dice game for two players with simple rules.

Rules:

  • If the player rolls number from 2 to 6, it is added to their turn points and player can continue turn.
  • After rolls player can click hold and add turn points to score.
  • If roll 1, player lose turn point and it start the next player's turn.
  • The first player who gain 100 points win game.
